Due - Simple Todo Countdown

Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2, 2024

Overview

Due ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data.

All your tasks, settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device only. We have no servers, no accounts, and no way to access your data.

Local Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Your todo items and their due dates
  • Trashed items (until you permanently delete them)
  • Your theme preference (light/dark/system)
  • Your font preference

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any external servers.

Advertising

The free version of Due displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. Google may collect certain data for ad personalization as described in their privacy policy.

You can learn more about Google's data practices here: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Due Pro (the paid version) does not contain any advertisements.

Third-Party Services

The app may use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob (free version only) - for displaying advertisements
  • Expo - for app updates and crash reporting (no personal data collected)

Children's Privacy

Due does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. The app is suitable for all ages (rated 4+).

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, security is managed by your device's built-in security features. We recommend keeping your device software up to date.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
