Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 25, 2026

Overview

To-Do Countdown ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data.

All your tasks, settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device only. We have no servers, no accounts, and no way to access your data.

Local Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Your todo items and their due dates
  • Trashed items (until you permanently delete them)
  • Your theme preference (light/dark/system)
  • Your font preference
  • Your countdown window and precision settings

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any external servers.

Notifications

To-Do Countdown uses local notifications to remind you about upcoming task deadlines. These notifications are scheduled by your device's operating system and do not involve any external servers or push services.

Children's Privacy

To-Do Countdown does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. The app is suitable for all ages (rated 4+).

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, security is managed by your device's built-in security features. We recommend keeping your device software up to date.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
